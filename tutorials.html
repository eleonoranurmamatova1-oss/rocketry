Water Rockets – Introduction

PHOTO

Water rockets are powered by compressed air and water. They are safe and ideal for learning aerodynamics.

Materials

Plastic bottles, fins, nose cone, launcher.

How to Build

Cut fins, attach symmetrically, seal all joints.

Launch

Pressurize gradually and keep safe distance.

Solid Fuel Rockets – Introduction

PHOTO

Solid fuel rockets use certified engines and require strict safety rules.

Safety

Never modify engines. Always use protection.

Construction

Focus on weight balance and stability.

Flight

Use parachute systems for safe landing.
