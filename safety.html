Safety Rules

Follow these rules to ensure a safe and enjoyable rocket experience.

  • Always wear protective goggles and gloves.
  • Never launch rockets near people or animals.
  • Use only certified engines and materials.
  • Keep a safe distance from launch area.
  • Check all connections and structures before launch.
  • Follow all instructions for water and solid fuel rockets.
  • Supervise children at all times.
  • Have fire safety equipment ready when using solid fuel.
  • Do not modify engines or propulsion systems.
  • Respect the environment and clean up after launches.
