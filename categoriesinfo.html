Rocket Categories

Choose your category based on experience, age and engineering level.

PHOTO

Water Rockets

Water rockets are powered by compressed air and water. This category is ideal for beginners and younger participants.

  • No combustion
  • Safe and educational
  • Focus on aerodynamics
PHOTO

Amateur Solid Fuel

Amateur solid fuel rockets introduce real propulsion systems while maintaining strict safety standards.

  • Basic propulsion physics
  • Structural calculations
  • Parachute or safe landing system
PHOTO

Sport Solid Fuel

The most advanced category for experienced teams. Focused on precision, performance and engineering excellence.

  • High altitude flights
  • Advanced stability systems
  • Professional competition standards
